About

The short version.

I’m Abubakr — a CS student at UC Irvine who cares about the parts most people don’t notice: the easing on a transition, the latency of a tap, the wording of an empty state.

I build for iOS in Swift and SwiftUI, and I’m comfortable across the wider stack. I like small teams, sharp constraints, and tools that respect the person holding them.

When I’m not shipping, I’m probably taking something apart to see how it was put together.
